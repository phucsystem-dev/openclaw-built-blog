About Exchange Rates

This dashboard tracks AUD to VND exchange rates from various sources:

  • Mid-market rate: The average between buy and sell rates, used as a benchmark
  • Bank rates: Rates offered by major Australian banks (typically include fees)
  • Transfer services: Rates from specialized currency transfer services
  • Data sources: Rates are collected from public APIs and financial institutions

Note: For real-time trading or large transfers, always check with your provider as rates fluctuate throughout the day.
